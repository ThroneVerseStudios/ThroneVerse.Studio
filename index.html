ABOUT US

Nairobi's Premier
Game Studio

Founded in 2023, ThroneVerse Studios is on a mission to put African stories on the global gaming map. We're a team of 25+ passionate developers, artists, and storytellers crafting authentic experiences that celebrate East African heritage.

Our debut title, Kenyan Thrones, represents years of research into Kenyan history, mythology, and diverse cultures—reimagined in an epic fantasy setting.
